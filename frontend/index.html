Model Assumptions
  • Corridor risk maps linearly to available loading capacity at that corridor.
  • Single-source concentration capped at 35% of total demand, reflecting real contractual diversification limits.
  • Refineries are held to a 92% minimum utilization floor; floor relaxes only when physically infeasible.
  • Grade substitution penalties are static estimates, not live assay-based costs.

Why This, Not a Chatbot Wrapper
